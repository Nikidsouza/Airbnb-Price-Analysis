Conclusion and Recommendations:¶

Conclusion:¶

  • Higher number of bedrooms, bathrooms and number of accommodates increase the log price of the property.
  • Customers are willing to pay a premium for well-reviewed properties.
  • Even adding one extra bathroom often increases the rental value noticeably.
  • Larger properties are charging higher prices.
  • The model shows moderate variance.

Recommendation¶

  • Since property size reflected through accommodates, bedrooms, beds, and bathrooms has a substantial impact on price, Airbnb should encourage hosts to optimize their space usage, add additional beds where feasible, and highlight these capacity-related features prominently in listings.
  • Airbnb should ensure hosts update all property details for more accurate pricing and better search ranking.
